Where Design Meets Code: UX/UI Designer, Artist, Front-end Developer

Crafting beautiful and functional user experiences.

About

I am a frontend developer with a passion for art and design. Over the past few years, I have honed my skills in various design tools including Figma, Canva, Adobe XD, and Sketch. My expertise extends to implementing UX and UI principles to create intuitive and beautiful web interfaces.

I believe in the power of design to solve problems and create meaningful user experiences. My approach to design is user-centered, ensuring that every project I work on meets the needs of its users while also achieving business goals.

When I am not designing or coding, you can find me exploring art galleries, trying out new design tools, or sketching out new ideas for future projects.

Projects

Little Lemon Restaurant

Little Lemon, a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, sought to revamp their online presence by developing a user-friendly and visually appealing website. My role as a front-end developer was to translate design mockups into a functional and responsive website that would showcase their menu, ambiance, and unique selling points.

Lucky Shurb

Lucky Shrub was started by Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing and communications. She is the go-to person for all things business-related. From product management and marketing to customer support and team management, Maria handles it all.

Trek Tide

Trek Tide, a dynamic travel agency specializing in adventure tours, sought a website that reflected their energetic spirit and streamlined the booking process. My role as a front-end developer was to build a user-friendly and visually engaging website using Vue.js and Tailwind CSS. The goal was to showcase Trek Tide's unique travel packages, simplify navigation, and encourage online bookings.
